FIT AS A FIDDLE!

About five years ago, I began learning the violin and took quite a liking to it. After learning to sight read from books, I moved to learning old-timey fiddle tunes from the internet. I began to frequent old-time jams and play in church, and I continue to play and enjoy fiddle music to this very day. While I still play with friends, or at an occasional chili dinner, I mostly practice for personal enjoyment. It proves to be a wonderful reliever of stress.

I am currently a sophomore student at Cedarville University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I anticipate graduation in May of 2020.
